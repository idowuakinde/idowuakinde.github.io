My Interests

My personal interests include:

  • Blockchain. I'm - quite literally - fascinated by the potential of business-oriented blockchain to rewrite global technology infrastructure, particularly in the developing world (like African countries) where such infrastructure is virtually non-existent. The business benefits of blockchain include trust (facilitated through immutability, transparency and traceability), efficiency and security.
  • Cybersecurity. 21st-century human life is probably unimaginable without technology. Yet, not a day passes without a significant news report covering a new cyber breach. I have a few ideas about how we might go about addressing this shortfall.
  • Virtual Reality. Having been a somewhat-ardent "first-person" game fanatic since childhood and having experienced immersive VR technology firsthand, I believe strongly that eXtended Reality (the combination of Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Mixed Reality) will play a huge part in the future of how humanity acquires skills (aka education).
